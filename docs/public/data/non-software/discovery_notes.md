# Discovery Notes - Perfect Disney Vacation

Project Slug: PERFECT-DISNEY-VACATION
Date: 2026-06-02

## 1. Context & Objectives

- **Domain**: Vacation planning / Travel itinerary.
- **Goal**: Plan a "perfect" 4-day vacation to Walt Disney World Florida for a family of 4.
- **Deliverables**: A combination of:
  - Day-by-day travel itinerary.
  - Budget breakdown.
  - Logistics and booking guide.

## 2. Locked Requirements

- **Travel Party**: Family of 4 (2 adults, a 7-year-old child, and a toddler who requires a midday nap).
- **Travel Dates**: June 15 – June 18, 2026.
  - June 15: Arrival at MCO at 9:00 AM.
  - June 18: Departure from MCO at 9:00 PM.
- **Lodging**: Disney's Port Orleans Resort - Riverside (On-property, Moderate resort).
- **Ticket Type**: 4-day Park Hopper tickets.
- **Pacing**: Slow-paced, flexible, incorporating a midday nap at the resort (Option A: return to Port Orleans Riverside for a quiet bed nap).
- **Airport Transportation**: Rideshare (Lyft/Uber) from MCO to Resort and back. [REVISED in Refinement Cycle 1]
  - _Safety/Logistics Rule_: The 7-year-old child is tall and does not require a booster seat. Only the toddler requires a car seat. The family will book the rideshare option that provides one car seat (e.g., Uber Car Seat), meaning they do not need to bring car seats from home.
- **In-Resort/Park Transportation**: Complimentary Disney transport:
  - Buses connect Port Orleans Riverside to all four theme parks and park-to-park.
  - Boat/Water Taxi connects the resort to Disney Springs.
  - Skyliner connects EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.
  - Monorail connects Magic Kingdom and EPCOT.
- **Lightning Lane**: Yes, buy Lightning Lane Multi Pass (and single passes where appropriate for major headliners).
- **Dining**: Focus on quick service, but include exactly 1 table-service meal per day that is moderately priced and themed to the day's location.
- **Budget**: Moderate tier.
- **Attractions & Interests**:
  - Focus franchises: Toy Story, Frozen, Disney Princesses, Mickey Mouse, Moana.
  - Exclude: Star Wars (not of interest yet) and Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind (skipped to focus on gentler family-wide rides) [REVISED in Refinement Cycle 1].

## 3. Recommended Itinerary Flow & Dining

- **Day 1 (Arrival, June 15)**: **EPCOT** (Late Afternoon/Evening) [REVISED in Refinement Cycle 1]
  - _Itinerary_: Arrive MCO 9:00 AM, check in/drop bags at Port Orleans Riverside 10:30 AM, resort lunch, toddler resort bed nap (1:00 PM – 3:30 PM), head to EPCOT at 4:00 PM.
  - _Highlights_: Remy's Ratatouille Adventure (using pre-booked Tier 1 LL), Spaceship Earth / Nemo (using Tier 2 LLs).
  - _Table Service_: **Garden Grill** (Character dining with Mickey and friends, family-style, moderate).
- **Day 2 (June 16)**: **Magic Kingdom** (Full Day with Midday Nap)
  - _Highlights_: Fantasyland (Peter Pan, Winnie the Pooh, Dumbo), Tomorrowland (Buzz Lightyear), Adventureland (Jungle Cruise).
  - _Table Service_: **The Crystal Palace** (Character dining with Winnie the Pooh, buffet, moderate-to-premium but directly in the park) or **The Plaza Restaurant** (Casual, moderate).
- **Day 3 (June 17)**: **Animal Kingdom** (Morning) $\rightarrow$ **Hollywood Studios** (Afternoon/Evening with Midday Nap in between)
  - _Highlights (AK)_: Kilimanjaro Safaris, Na'vi River Journey, Festival of the Lion King.
  - _Highlights (HS)_: Toy Story Land (Toy Story Mania, Alien Swirling Saucers, Slinky Dog Dash if the 7yo meets the 38" height limit), Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway.
  - _Table Service_: **Sci-Fi Dine-In Theater** (Hollywood Studios - retro car tables, very popular and moderately priced).
- **Day 4 (Departure, June 18)**: **EPCOT & Magic Kingdom / Departure** (Morning/Afternoon) [REVISED in Refinement Cycle 1]
  - _Itinerary_: Checkout of room at 8:30 AM, drop bags at Bell Services, head to EPCOT in the morning and Magic Kingdom in the afternoon for favorites.
  - _Highlights_: Frozen Ever After (using pre-booked Tier 1 LL at EPCOT to avoid lines), re-riding favorites, and souvenir shopping.
  - _Nap Strategy_: Toddler naps in the stroller in the park or in a quiet area of the resort/lobby from 1:00 PM to 3:30 PM (stroller exception day).
  - _Table Service_: **Beignets at Port Orleans French Quarter** (Quick/casual breakfast) and a lunch at **The Plaza Restaurant** (Magic Kingdom) or **Grand Floridian Cafe** (short monorail ride from MK).
  - _Departure_: Return to resort at 5:00 PM to collect bags, request rideshare at 5:30 PM to MCO for a 9:00 PM flight.
