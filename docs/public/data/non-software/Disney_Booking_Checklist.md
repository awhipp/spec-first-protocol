# Disney World Vacation Booking Checklist

Use this timeline to ensure all booking windows and daily tasks are completed on time for your trip starting **June 15, 2026**.

## Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Prior to 60 Days Out)

- [ ] **Purchase 4-Day Park Hopper Tickets**: Link them to all family member profiles in the My Disney Experience app.
- [ ] **Verify Resort Reservation**: Ensure Disney's Port Orleans Resort - Riverside reservation is linked and visible in the app.
- [ ] **Coordinate Stroller**: Decide on renting a high-quality stroller (e.g., from an off-property service like Kingdom Strollers, which delivers to Riverside bell services) or bringing your own.

---

## Phase 2: 60 Days Out (April 16, 2026 at 6:00 AM EST)

_Highly recommended to book right at 6:00 AM EST as character dining slots fill instantly._

- [ ] **Book Day 1 Dinner**: Garden Grill Restaurant (EPCOT) - character dining.
- [ ] **Book Day 2 Dinner/Lunch**: The Crystal Palace (Magic Kingdom) - character dining.
- [ ] **Book Day 3 Dinner**: Sci-Fi Dine-In Theater (Hollywood Studios).
- [ ] **Book Day 4 Lunch**: The Plaza Restaurant (Magic Kingdom) or Grand Floridian Cafe.

---

## Phase 3: 7 Days Out (June 8, 2026 at 7:00 AM EST)

_As an on-property resort guest, your window opens 7 days before check-in for the duration of your stay._

- [ ] **Purchase Lightning Lane Multi Pass** for all 4 days.
- [ ] **Pre-Book Day 1 Lightning Lanes** (EPCOT):
  - [ ] _Remy's Ratatouille Adventure_ (Tier 1) - target late afternoon (e.g., 4:30 PM).
  - [ ] _Spaceship Earth_ (Tier 2)
  - [ ] _The Seas with Nemo & Friends_ (Tier 2)
- [ ] **Pre-Book Day 2 Lightning Lanes** (Magic Kingdom):
  - [ ] _Peter Pan's Flight_ (Tier 1 or high-priority Tier 2 equivalent depending on 2026 rules - otherwise pick _Jungle Cruise_).
  - [ ] _Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh_
  - [ ] _Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin_
- [ ] **Pre-Book Day 3 Lightning Lanes**:
  - [ ] _Slinky Dog Dash_ (Tier 1 at Hollywood Studios) - target late afternoon (e.g., 5:00 PM) for the 7yo and adults (use Rider Switch).
  - [ ] _Toy Story Mania_ (Tier 2 at Hollywood Studios)
  - [ ] _Na'vi River Journey_ (Animal Kingdom morning) - _Note: If booking park-to-park, select your morning Animal Kingdom rides first._
- [ ] **Pre-Book Day 4 Lightning Lanes** (EPCOT/Magic Kingdom):
  - [ ] _Frozen Ever After_ (Tier 1 at EPCOT) - target morning (e.g., 9:30 AM).
  - [ ] _Dumbo the Flying Elephant_ (Magic Kingdom afternoon)
  - [ ] _Under the Sea - Journey of the Little Mermaid_ (Magic Kingdom afternoon)

---

## Phase 4: Night Before & Arrival Day

- [ ] **Complete Online Check-In**: Submit room preferences and credit card info in the Disney app.
- [ ] **Pack Day-Pack/Diaper Bag**: Keep sunscreen, rain ponchos, hats, change of clothes, and toddler snacks easily accessible (do not pack in checked luggage).
- [ ] **Request MCO Rideshare**: Order an **Uber Car Seat** (or Lyft equivalent) upon landing at 9:00 AM. Confirm it has one child car seat installed.
- [ ] **Drop bags at Bell Services**: Upon arrival at Port Orleans Riverside, drop checked bags so you can go straight to your room for the nap or head out.

---

## Phase 5: Daily Park Operations Checklist

- [ ] **At 7:00 AM Daily**: Open My Disney Experience app to check ride statuses, modify any LL times if needed, or buy LL passes if not pre-purchased.
- [ ] **Midday Nap Handoff**: At 12:00 PM, head to the bus stop. Check the app for the next bus to Port Orleans Riverside.
- [ ] **Next LL Booking**: Every time you scan into a Lightning Lane ride, immediately open the app to book your next available ride.
