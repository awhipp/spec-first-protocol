# Disney World Family Packing List (Family of 4)

A curated checklist tailored for your 4-day trip in June (hot, humid, and rainy season in Florida) with a toddler and a 7-year-old.

## 1. Park Bag (Carry-On/Essentials)

*These items should be in a lightweight backpack or diaper bag that stays with you at all times.*

- [ ] **Sun protection**: SPF 50+ sunscreen (spray and face sticks), sunglasses for everyone, wide-brimmed hats.
- [ ] **Rain gear**: 4 lightweight reusable rain ponchos (buying in parks is expensive) + a plastic rain cover specifically for the stroller.
- [ ] **Cooling gear**: 2 rechargeable neck fans or handheld misting fans, cooling towels.
- [ ] **Electronics**: 2 high-capacity portable power banks (for phones running the Disney app and Lightning Lanes all day) + charging cables.
- [ ] **Toddler essentials**: Diapers/wipes, diaper disposal bags, change of clothes (accidents happen), pacifiers, small quiet toys for ride queues.
- [ ] **Snacks & Hydration**: Reusable insulated water bottles (fill with free ice water at quick-service locations), toddler-friendly snacks (goldfish, fruit pouches, granola bars).
- [ ] **First Aid**: Bandaids, blister pads (Moleskin), kids' Tylenol/Motrin, hand sanitizer, sanitizing wipes for tables and high chairs.

---

## 2. Clothing & Footwear

*June in Orlando is very hot (highs ~90°F) and rain is expected daily.*

- [ ] **Footwear (Critical)**: 2 pairs of broken-in, comfortable walking shoes for adults and the 7yo (one pair that can dry quickly if it rains, or waterproof sandals like Keens/Teva). Comfortable sandals/shoes for the toddler.
- [ ] **Outfits**: 5 lightweight, moisture-wicking daily outfits per person (pack extra shirts due to sweat/humidity).
- [ ] **Swimwear**: 2 swimsuits per person + swim diapers for the toddler (required for resort pools) + goggles for the 7yo.
- [ ] **Light layers**: 1 light sweater or jacket per person (indoor queues and restaurants have very cold air conditioning).
- [ ] **Pajamas**: Comfortable, breathable sleepwear.

---

## 3. Stroller Essentials

- [ ] **Stroller**: Bring a comfortable, reclining single stroller (toddler can nap in it on departure day) or rent one locally.
- [ ] **Stroller Fan**: Clip-on, battery-operated fan (vital for keeping the toddler cool in the humidity).
- [ ] **Stroller Identifier**: A bright ribbon, balloon, or luggage tag to locate your stroller in the sea of parked strollers.
- [ ] **Stroller Hook**: A carabiner/hook (like "The Mommy Hook") to hang shopping bags or diaper bags.

---

## 4. Flight & Airport Travel

- [ ] **Toddler flight activities**: Coloring books, tablet loaded with offline videos, headphones.
- [ ] **Identification**: Driver's licenses/IDs for adults, flight boarding passes.
- [ ] **Personal Items**: Hand wipes, tissues, empty water bottles to fill past security.
- [ ] *Note: You do not need to pack booster seats or toddler car seats, as you are using the rideshare "Car Seat" tier for the airport transfers!*
