## 1. Overview

This specification details the plan, logistics, and constraints for a 4-day Disney World vacation for a family of 4. The goal is to provide a stress-free, slow-paced vacation that accommodates the needs of a toddler requiring a midday nap, while still allowing the 7-year-old child and adults to experience top attractions and character interactions.

## 2. Logistics
- **Dates**: June 15, 2026 - June 18, 2026
- **Destinations**: Walt Disney World (Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom), Port Orleans Resort - Riverside.
- **Transit Modes**: 
  - Flights into MCO.
  - Rideshare (Uber/Lyft Car Seat tier) to and from the airport.
  - Complimentary Disney buses and water taxis on property.

## 3. Requirements & Documents
- **Passports/Visas Required**: N/A (Domestic travel). Real ID required for domestic flights.
- **Health/Vaccinations**: No special requirements. Standard travel health precautions.

## 4. Constraints
- **Budget Maximum**: Moderate Tier (Accommodation at Port Orleans, mix of Quick Service and Table Service dining).
- **Accessibility Needs**: 
  - Stroller required for the 2-3 year old toddler. Must be foldable for transit.
  - Strict midday nap requirement (1:00 PM – 3:30 PM).
  - 7-year-old is tall enough to not require a booster seat in rideshares, and meets 38" to 40" ride height requirements.
- **Dietary Restrictions**: None specified. 

## 5. Itinerary (Run-of-Show)

### Day 1: Arrival & EPCOT (June 15)
- **09:00 AM**: Arrive MCO Airport. Collect baggage.
- **09:45 AM**: Board rideshare (Uber/Lyft Car Seat tier).
- **10:30 AM**: Arrive at Port Orleans Riverside. Check in online, drop luggage.
- **11:30 AM – 01:00 PM**: Quick-service lunch at Riverside Mill Food Court.
- **01:00 PM – 03:30 PM**: **Resort Bed Nap**: Toddler naps in hotel. Parents/7yo rest or swim.
- **04:00 PM**: Bus to EPCOT.
- **04:30 PM**: Ride Remy's Ratatouille Adventure (Tier 1 LL). Spaceship Earth (Tier 2 LL).
- **06:30 PM**: **Table-Service Dinner**: Garden Grill Restaurant.
- **08:30 PM**: Return to resort via bus.

### Day 2: Magic Kingdom Full Day (June 16)
- **08:00 AM**: Board bus to Magic Kingdom (Early Entry at 8:30 AM).
- **08:30 AM – 12:00 PM**: Fantasyland and Tomorrowland.
- **12:00 PM**: Travel back to Port Orleans Riverside.
- **12:30 PM**: Quick-service lunch at Sassagoula Floatworks.
- **01:00 PM – 03:30 PM**: **Resort Bed Nap**.
- **04:00 PM**: Bus back to Magic Kingdom.
- **04:30 PM – 06:30 PM**: Adventureland touring using LL.
- **06:30 PM**: **Table-Service Dinner**: The Crystal Palace.
- **08:00 PM**: Happily Ever After fireworks.
- **09:30 PM**: Return to resort via bus.

### Day 3: Animal Kingdom & Hollywood Studios (June 17)
- **08:00 AM**: Board bus to Animal Kingdom.
- **08:30 AM – 11:30 AM**: Kilimanjaro Safaris, Na'vi River Journey, Festival of the Lion King.
- **11:30 AM**: Quick-service lunch at Satu'li Canteen.
- **12:15 PM**: Bus back to Port Orleans Riverside.
- **01:00 PM – 03:30 PM**: **Resort Bed Nap**.
- **04:00 PM**: Bus to Disney's Hollywood Studios.
- **04:30 PM – 06:30 PM**: Toy Story Land (use Rider Switch for Slinky Dog Dash).
- **06:45 PM**: **Table-Service Dinner**: Sci-Fi Dine-In Theater Restaurant.
- **08:30 PM**: Fantasmic! night show.

### Day 4: Departure (June 18)
- **08:30 AM**: Check out of room; leave bags with Bell Services.
- **09:00 AM**: Bus to EPCOT for morning favorites (Frozen Ever After LL).
- **12:00 PM**: Hop to Magic Kingdom via Monorail.
- **01:00 PM**: **Table-Service Lunch**: The Plaza Restaurant.
- **02:00 PM – 03:30 PM**: **Stroller Nap (Departure Exception)**.
- **04:30 PM**: Bus back to Port Orleans Riverside.
- **05:30 PM**: Request rideshare (Uber/Lyft Car Seat tier) from resort to MCO Airport.
- **09:00 PM**: Departure flight.

## 6. Contingency Matrix
- **Weather Delays**: Pack rain ponchos and stroller covers. Move to indoor attractions (e.g., Epcot pavilions, Mickey's PhilharMagic) if outdoor rides close due to Florida storms.
- **Cancellations / Flight Delays**: If June 15 flight is delayed past 4:00 PM, cancel Garden Grill dinner (Disney allows cancellation up to 2 hours prior without fee).
- **Toddler Meltdown / Refusal to Nap**: Do not force the park return if they fall asleep in the stroller. Use quiet spots in the parks (e.g., Baby Care Centers) for downtime.
- **Missed Lightning Lane**: Disney automatically converts the missed LL into a "Multi-Experience Pass" valid for almost any other ride in the park.
